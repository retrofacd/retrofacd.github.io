My Personal Take on Beauty and Perfection

I had come to comprehend the necessity of imagination, knowledge, and perseverance at a young age in the forest. I found, there is a time and place for heavy or strong branches at the bottom of a structure; it is these pieces that hold the guidance and strength for the majority of the other branches, which, by a combination of nature and nurture, happened to be more vulnerable and malleable. Lastly, there is a place and function for the flexibility and durability of ivy in that it secures and utilizes the best of the branches combined ability to overcome adversity together. Thus I understood the significance and beauty of imperfections and differences in individuals as they also have the ability to work together and achieve their highest potential.

It is an essential quality in any field of study to fully comprehend the complexities and differences that make up the identity of an individual. Furthermore, the genetic code and the social influence of a human being is far too varied in order to justify acts of segregation or inequality. Those who categorize others based solely on physical appearance and gender fail to understand this seemingly obvious notion. With that said, not one individual can be blinded or freed from the instances of discrimination; however, it is my duty and passion to weaken the confinements that other human beings place on one another. To view differences as strengths requires an imaginative and a compassionate mind. I strive to place the values of imagination, knowledge and perseverance, in a productive setting in hopes of ... to ultimately help other individual’s lives and thier connection with technology.

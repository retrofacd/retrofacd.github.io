My Personal Take on Beauty and Perfection

I had come to comprehend the necessity of imagination, knowledge, and perseverance at a young age in the forest. I found, there is a time and place for heavy or strong branches at the bottom of a structure; it is these pieces that hold the guidance and strength for the majority of the other branches, which, by a combination of nature and nurture, happened to be more vulnerable and malleable. Lastly, there is a place and function for the flexibility and durability of ivy in that it secures and utilizes the best of the branches combined ability to overcome adversity together. Thus I understood the significance and beauty of imperfections and differences in individuals as they also have the ability to work together and achieve their highest potential. To view differences as strengths requires an imaginative and a compassionate mind however if we do, we will understand true beauty.
